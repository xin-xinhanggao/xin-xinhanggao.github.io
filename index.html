About Me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Shi-Min Hu and Prof. Shing-Tung Yau. I received my bachelor degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in 2018.

My research focuses on physically-based rendering rendering, real-time rendering and differentiable rendering.

Projects

Jrender: A differentiable rendering library based on jittor.

Jrender aims to provide an effecient differentiable rendering library for research. Now we support mesh\point clouds\volumes rendering, and kinds of appearance models such as diffuse, glossy, metallic, glass and subsurface models. Due to the speedup of jittor, Jrender is times faster than other differentiable rendering library.

[Github]

Raytracing Subsurface Scattering based on UE5.

Subsurface scattering models play an important role in realistic rendering, specially for materials such as skin, wax, etc. We use real-time raytracing to simulate the scattering and absorption in the model based on UE5. Compared with the screen space methods, our method reaches a better quality and achieves interactive framerates.
